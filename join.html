Become a Mentee!

Don’t miss out on the fun! Apply between 6/25 and 7/30!

Becoming a mentee is easy, and practical. Mentees in the program are connected with a mentor who they will have access to for personal, one-on-one sessions. Any first year student or incoming freshman can apply.

Become a Mentor!

Contribute to the community!

We are always looking for mentors who can take the lead and help guide new students. Mentors must be freshman, sophomores, or juniors and have a current major in the Information Systems program.
